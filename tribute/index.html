George Washington

Timeline

1732 - Born in Westmoreland County, Virginia.

1753 - Delivered a message to the French, sparking the French and Indian War.

1759 - Married Martha Custis and gained social status.

1775 - Chosen to lead the Continental Army in the Revolutionary War.

1781 - Led American forces to victory at the Battle of Yorktown.

1787 - Presided over the Constitutional Convention.

1789 - Elected unanimously as the first U.S. President.

1797 - Retired from the presidency after two terms.

1799 - Passed away at Mount Vernon.
